01 / Philosophy

The Core Belief

Society tells you friction is bad. We say friction is the only reason anything moves at all.

Manifesto
"You can't have forward progression without friction. It's like trying to run on an ice rink with a pair of Jordans on. It's just not happening."
The Problem
People treat friction like an enemy. They avoid it. Run from it. But every entrepreneur, creative, and builder knows the truth: the resistance is where the growth happens. Without friction, you're just sliding. You're not going anywhere.
The Reframe
Friction is the grip that lets you push forward. We wouldn't be able to walk, sit, stand, or build anything without it. The podcast exists to normalize the struggle and show people that the hard parts aren't the problem. They're the proof you're actually moving.

06 / Voice & Tone

How It Speaks

Casual, direct, sincere. Sounds like a real conversation with someone who's been through it and came out better.

The Brand Does
  • Talk like a real person in a real conversation
  • Use everyday language, no corporate speak
  • Be direct and say what it means
  • Encourage without being preachy
  • Share real stories and real struggle
  • Make the complicated feel simple
  • Stay curious and ask the uncomfortable questions
The Brand Doesn't
  • Use jargon, buzzwords, or business-bro energy
  • Lecture or talk down to the audience
  • Pretend the hard parts don't exist
  • Sound polished, formal, or corporate
  • Make sweeping statements without backing them up
  • Chase trends or mimic other podcast brands
  • Use negativity or pessimism as a hook
Example: Intro Copy
Welcome to the Friction Podcast, your premier destination for entrepreneurial thought leadership and innovative mindset strategies.
vs.
Look, nobody said building something would be easy. We're here because the hard parts are the parts that actually matter. That resistance you're feeling? That's friction. And friction is the only reason anything moves.
Example: Social Post
Unlock your potential by embracing challenges! Subscribe to our podcast for expert insights on overcoming obstacles. #Mindset #Entrepreneurship
vs.
Everyone tells you to avoid friction. But have you ever tried to run on ice? You go nowhere. The grip is what moves you forward. New episode drops tomorrow.
