What This Is

Friction is not the enemy.

Society tells you to avoid friction. We say it's the only reason anything moves. You can't run on ice. You need grip. That's what this podcast is about.

⚡
Real Conversations
No scripts. No corporate talking points. Just honest conversations with people who are actually building things and hitting walls and pushing through them.
🧠
Mindset First
Before the strategy, before the tools, before the funding. Your head has to be right. We talk about the mental game that nobody else wants to talk about.
🔥
Built for Builders
Entrepreneurs in their 20s figuring it out. Business owners in their 40s leveling up. Anyone who knows the struggle is where the growth happens.
"You can't have forward progression without friction. It's like trying to run on an ice rink with Jordans on. It's just not happening."
- Joon, Founder
